## Quick primer — non-negotiables

- **Language:** everything in Danish. Informal `du`-form, club-first plural (`vi`, `foreningen`).
- **Tone:** practical, hospitable, no hype. Sentence case everywhere. No emoji.
- **Palette:** Forest `#1E2A22`, Bone `#F4EFE4`, Brass `#B8893A` (accent), Clay `#C7502E` (danger/live).
- **Type:** Fraunces (display) + Inter (body/UI) + JetBrains Mono (results/numerics).
- **Icons:** Lucide 1.75-stroke + the 2 custom hunting glyphs in `assets/` (clay pigeon, shotshell).
- **Audience:** hunters 25–55 primarily, but many older members — prioritise legibility, hit targets ≥ 44px, no hover-only UI.
- **Do not** recreate the current WordPress Minamaze theme — HOJ has asked for a fresh redesign.
